## Final Notes for Submission

### App Store Connect

1. Use **`Tatum: Intimate Wellness Diary`** as the app name.
2. Primary Category: **Health & Fitness**, Secondary: **Lifestyle**.
3. Age Rating: **17+** with **"Infrequent/Mild Sexual Content"**.
4. **Do not** use the words `sex`, `sexual`, or `erotic` in the title, subtitle, or keyword field — Apple will flag for review.
5. The keyword field must be exactly as written — no spaces after commas, no repeated words from the title.
6. Screenshot overlays should be clean and minimal — use Tatum's Sand and Fig color palette.

### Google Play Console

1. Content Rating: select **"Mature 17+"** during the rating questionnaire.
2. Google is more lenient than Apple on terminology, but stay consistent with the wellness positioning.
3. The short description is the most important field for Google Play search — keep it exactly as written.
